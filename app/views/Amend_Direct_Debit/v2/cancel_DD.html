When not to cancel your Direct Debit

If you are moving home, contact the council's council tax team.

If you are no longer responsible for a property, contact the council's council tax team.

If you wish to pay the Direct Debit from a different bank account you can change the bank account details.

When to cancel your Direct Debit

If you are want to continue paying council tax for the property but you do not want the payment to automatically leave a bank account you have chosen.
