Set up a council tax Direct Debit online

Before you start

You will need

  • Your council tax account number, found on any Royal Borough of Greenwich council tax bill
  • Your bank account details. You must be the sole signatory and it must be a UK bank account.
  • Need to know whether you want to pay your council tax for a year in one amount or a number of months
  • An idea of a day of the month you want your Direct Debit to leave your bank account
