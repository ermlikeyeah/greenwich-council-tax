Connect to a council tax account

To manage your council tax online we need to connect to your council tax account.

This process should take several minutes.

Before you start

You will need:

  • A recent council tax bill for the property.
  • The month and year you became liable for paying council tax for the property. This may be the same as the date you legally took ownership of the property.
